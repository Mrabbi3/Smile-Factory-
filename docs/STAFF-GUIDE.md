# The Smile Factory - Staff Portal Guide

A quick-start guide for owners, managers, and employees who use the Staff Portal
every day. No technical knowledge needed.

---

## Getting Into the Staff Portal

### First Time Setup

Your manager or owner will give you two things:
1. **Staff PIN** -- a code that unlocks the staff area
2. **Your login email and password** -- your personal account

### Logging In

1. Go to the website and click **Staff** (top-right corner of the navigation bar)
2. Enter the **Staff PIN** and click Submit
3. You'll be taken to the login page -- enter your **email and password**
4. You're in! The Dashboard appears automatically

> The Staff PIN stays active for 8 hours (one shift). If you get logged out,
> just enter the PIN and your email/password again.

### Logging Out

Click your avatar or name in the top-right corner of the staff portal and
select **Sign Out**.

---

## Dashboard

The Dashboard is your home screen. It shows:
- **Today's revenue** -- how much money came in today (cash and card)
- **Recent sales** -- the last few token transactions
- **Alerts** -- things that need attention (low prize stock, upcoming bookings, open work orders)
- **Quick Actions** -- shortcuts to common tasks

---

## Selling Tokens (POS)

This is the most common daily task.

1. Click **POS / Token Sales** in the sidebar
2. Choose the token amount the customer wants ($1, $5, $10, or $20)
3. Tap the amount to add it to the cart -- you can add multiple items
4. Select **Cash** or **Card** as the payment method
   - Card purchases have a **$10 minimum** (the system enforces this)
5. Click **Complete Sale**
6. A receipt is generated -- you can:
   - **Print** the PDF receipt
   - **Email** the receipt to the customer (if they have an account)

### Loyalty Deals ($100 Bonus)

For the $100 loyalty deal (gives bonus tokens), a **Manager or Owner** must be
logged in. Employees cannot process loyalty deals.

## Party Bookings

### Viewing Bookings

1. Click **Bookings** in the sidebar
2. You'll see all upcoming bookings with their status (Pending, Confirmed, Completed, Cancelled)

### Creating a New Booking

1. Click the **New Booking** button
2. Fill in the customer's contact info, party date/time, and package
3. Note the deposit amount -- parties require a **$100 deposit**
4. Click **Create Booking**
5. The system can send a confirmation email to the customer automatically

### Updating a Booking

Click on any booking to change its status, update details, or mark the deposit as paid.

> For parties with **more than 25 kids**, the system will prompt you to call the
> customer to discuss special arrangements.

---

## Prize Inventory

### Checking Stock

1. Click **Prize Inventory** in the sidebar
2. You'll see all prizes with their current stock levels and ticket costs
3. Prizes with low stock are highlighted

### Recording a Redemption

1. Find the prize a customer wants to redeem
2. Click **Redeem** on that prize
3. The stock count decreases automatically

### Adding or Editing Prizes (Managers/Owners Only)

Click **Add Prize** to create a new item, or click on an existing prize to edit its
name, ticket cost, stock, or image.

---

## Token Transactions

Click **Token Transactions** in the sidebar to see a log of all token sales.
This page shows:
- Transaction time and amount
- Payment method (cash or card)
- Which staff member processed the sale

---

## Machines & Maintenance

### Viewing Machines

Click **Machines** in the sidebar to see the full list of arcade machines
with their status and location.

### Reporting a Machine Issue

1. Click on a machine that needs attention
2. Create a **maintenance ticket** describing the problem
3. The ticket appears in the Work Orders list for follow-up

---

## Work Orders

Work orders track tasks that need to be done (repairs, maintenance, etc.).

1. Click **Work Orders** in the sidebar
2. Create a new work order with a description, priority (Low/Medium/High/Urgent), and optionally link it to a machine
3. Assign it to a staff member
4. Update the status as work progresses (Open, In Progress, Completed)

---

## Customers

Click **Customers** in the sidebar to see registered customer accounts.
You can view their spending history, loyalty tier, and contact information.

---

## Expenses (Owners Only)

Owners can track business expenses:

1. Click **Expenses** in the sidebar
2. Click **Add Expense** to log a new expense
3. Fill in the category (Maintenance, Supplies, Prizes, Utilities, Other), amount, payment method, and notes
4. View monthly expense summaries on the same page

---

## Loyalty & Coupons

### Loyalty Accounts

Click **Loyalty** to see customer loyalty accounts and their tiers:
- **Bronze** -- Starting tier
- **Silver** -- $200+ spent
- **Gold** -- $500+ spent
- **Platinum** -- $1,000+ spent

### Coupons

Click **Coupons** to create and manage promotional discount codes.
You can set discount amounts, validity dates, and usage limits.

---

## Reports & Documents (Owners Only)

### Reports

Click **Reports** for visual charts and summaries:
- Revenue by date range
- Inventory turnover
- Booking statistics
- Expense breakdowns

### Documents (Exporting Data)

Click **Documents** to generate downloadable files:
- **PDF reports** -- formatted for printing
- **Excel spreadsheets** -- for bookkeeping or sharing with an accountant

Select the report type, date range, and format, then click **Generate**.

---

## Settings (Owners Only)

Click **Settings** to manage business configuration values. Changes here affect
how the system behaves (e.g., operating hours, pricing rules).

---

## Employee Management (Managers/Owners Only)

Click **Employees** to see the staff directory. Owners can:
- Change an employee's role (Employee, Manager, Owner)
- Deactivate accounts for staff who have left

## Common Questions

**Q: I forgot my password.**
Go to the login page and click "Forgot Password." A reset link will be sent
to your email.

**Q: The Staff PIN isn't working.**
Ask your manager or owner -- the PIN may have been changed. The PIN is set
in the system configuration and can be updated at any time.

**Q: I made a mistake on a token sale.**
Contact your manager. Currently, sales cannot be undone in the system, but
the manager can note the correction in expenses or adjust records.

**Q: The system is slow or not loading.**
Try refreshing the page (Ctrl+R or Cmd+R). If the problem persists, check
your internet connection. If it's still broken, contact the developer.

**Q: How do I get help with the system?**
For day-to-day questions, ask your manager. For technical issues (the site is
down, something is broken), contact the developer who set up the system.
